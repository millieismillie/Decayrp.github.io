Rraid Eevidence

When raiding a base by destroying a player-built structure such as a wall or a door, or if you cut a lock to get in, you are required to make a raid evidence ticket in the discord. Please follow the steps listed below to ensure that you've left the proper evidence. Please note: sneaking in through vulnerabilities in a base (or cracking their code) without breaking a wall, door, or lock does not require a raid ticket, nor does dismantling a roadblock.

1. Take a screenshot of your location with the base visible and note the server time (EST).

2. Once you're somewhere safe, open a ticket and post the screencap.

3. Make a d20 roll in the ticket.

4. Consult the table below and pick your clues.

5. Make a post in the ticket detailing what clues you left behind.

Die Roll Clues Left
20 None
16-191 Clue
10-152 Clues
2-93 Clues
12 Clues + Personal item


example clues

The following list are examples of clues you could potentially leave behind. It’s not meant to be exhaustive, but to give you some basic ideas to pick from. If you come up with a clue that you think is better suited to the situation, you’re welcome to use it. Please just make sure your clues are actually clues.

Footprints Describe the type of shoe based on treads, as well as any mud, sand, water, blood, etc that they might have stepped in.

Tire Treads Describe the type of tire and vehicle based on treads.

Trail Visible trail in the direction raiders headed.

Bullets A round or two of a caliber used by the character, or potentially shell casings.

Fabric A ripped piece of clothing from their outfit, something that might be identifiable.

Litter Cigarette butts (include brand), roaches, empty bottles, candy bar wrappers, things left behind that have a connection with your character.


Personal item Something personally identifiable or heavily associated with your character or group. An engraved lighter, a journal, an armband. Whatever the item is, it should point directly toward you.

Iinvestigation

If you return to your base to find that walls, doors, or locks have been broken, you may make an Investigation ticket in the discord. This should include a screencap of your base, including whatever structure was broken, so that it can be matched to the Evidence ticket. Please also include the time EST that you found it broken into, and try to estimate the length of time since the last time you saw your base intact. If a corresponding raid evidence ticket exists, you will be given the clues provided by the trespasser. Please note that suspicion or even confirmation of a trespasser's identity does not automatically give you rights on them.
